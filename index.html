Online Security Checklist

Use these questions to assess your online security and protect your identity and information:

  • Have you enabled two-factor authentication (2FA) for your important accounts?
  • Are your passwords unique and strong for each online account?
  • Do you use a reputable password manager to store and generate strong passwords?
  • Have you changed your passwords in the past six months for critical accounts?
  • Do you double-check email sender addresses before clicking on links or downloading attachments?
  • Have you received any suspicious emails recently? If so, did you report them to IT or your security team?
  • Have you undergone any phishing awareness training?
  • Is your operating system and software up-to-date with the latest security patches?
  • Do you have antivirus and anti-malware software installed and updated?
  • Have you configured firewalls on your devices to block unauthorized access?
  • Have you reviewed and adjusted your social media privacy settings to limit the sharing of personal information?
  • Do you regularly review your friends/followers and remove any unknown or suspicious accounts?
  • Do you avoid oversharing personal information on social media?
  • Are you using encrypted messaging apps for sensitive conversations?
  • Have you encrypted your email communications, especially when sending sensitive information?
  • Do you use secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) for online transactions?
  • Is your mobile device protected with a strong PIN or password?
  • Do you use biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint or face recognition) when available?
  • Have you enabled remote tracking and wiping features in case your device is lost or stolen?
  • Do you avoid connecting to unsecured public Wi-Fi networks for sensitive tasks?
  • Have you set up and use a VPN (Virtual Private Network) when accessing the internet on public networks?
  • If you come across sensitive or confidential information accidentally, do you report it to the appropriate authorities?
  • Are you aware of your organization's data handling and sharing policies, and do you adhere to them?
  • If you find a vulnerability in a system, do you report it responsibly instead of exploiting it?
  • Do you regularly back up important data to a secure location?
  • Have you tested the restoration process to ensure you can recover your data if needed?
  • Have you considered using identity protection services that monitor your personal information for potential breaches?
